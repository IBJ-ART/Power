The Power Within

Power is a fundamental concept in many areas of our lives. It can refer to:

  • Energy: The ability to do work. We use various forms of power, like electricity, solar, or wind, to run appliances, lights, and more.
  • Authority: The right or ability to control or influence others. Leaders have power to make decisions that affect people.
  • Strength: Physical or mental force or capability. We need power to perform tasks, overcome challenges, and achieve goals.
